Welcome to HPP Browser Login

HPP replaces passwords and CAPTCHAs with cryptographic proof that a real human is present — using the hardware already built into your device.

When you log into an HPP-enabled site, your device's secure chip (Touch ID, Face ID, or Windows Hello) signs a time-limited challenge. The server receives proof of presence, not a password. Nothing biometric ever leaves your device.

How HPP Works

1
Site requests proof
An HPP-enabled site asks for presence verification instead of a password.
2
Your hardware signs
Your secure chip produces a hardware-bound signature. Your biometric never leaves the device.
3
Certificate is issued
A time-limited certificate proves your presence. The site trusts the proof — no password needed.
